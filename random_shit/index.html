Crude oil carried by in freight trains,
Is literally volatile,
Men in neon jackets pull empty oil drums From the reservoir.
Falcons and robins
Soar
Over the reservoir
And between trees,
Solar panels,
And land on antennas.

The oil drums are coated in water sludge. Purple lily pads float
Near the black Jeep on the shore.
